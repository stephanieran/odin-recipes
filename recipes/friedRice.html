Fried Rice

Description

Ingredients

  • 1 cup rice
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 1 chicken breast
  • 3 eggs
  • 1 head cabbage
  • Scallion

Directions

  1. Cook the rice
  2. While rice cooks, cook chicken and scramble eggs
  3. Add rice and soy sauce
  4. Stir
  5. Top with scallions and serve hot
